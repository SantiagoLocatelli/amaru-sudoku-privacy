Privacy Policy — Sudoku

Effective date: 2026-05-05
Developer: Santiago Locatelli
Contact: amarugts@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy describes how the Sudoku mobile application ("the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use it. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

What we collect

We collect only the information necessary to run the app's features.

Account information (when you sign in with Google):

  • Your Google email address
  • Your Google display name
  • A unique Google account identifier (UID)

We use this to authenticate you, save your progress, and show your name on leaderboards.

Gameplay data (saved when you complete a game):

  • Completion time, errors, hints used, difficulty
  • Whether the game was solo or multiplayer
  • The date you played

We use this for the global leaderboard and the daily challenge ranking.

Multiplayer match data (only while a versus-mode match is active):

  • Your account UID and your opponent's UID
  • The shared puzzle and current progress
  • Completion timestamps

We use this to run real-time matches between two players.

Advertising data (collected automatically by Google AdMob):

  • Your device's advertising identifier
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • Device model and operating system version
  • Ad interactions

Google uses this to deliver and measure ads. The app itself does not read or store this data — it is handled directly by Google's ad SDK.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your real name, phone number, or precise location.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.
  • We do not sell your data to anyone.

Third-party services

The app uses these services, each with their own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, databases, hosting. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — advertising. Google Ads Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — authentication. Google Privacy Policy

How long we keep your data

  • Account data and leaderboard scores: kept until you ask us to delete them.
  • Active match data: automatically deleted when a match ends.
  • Daily challenge entries: kept indefinitely as part of the public ranking.
  • Advertising data: retained by Google according to their policies, not by us.

Your rights

You can:

  • Sign out at any time from inside the app.
  • Reset your advertising identifier in your Android device settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads).
  • Request deletion of your account data, leaderboard entries, and daily challenge entries by emailing amarugts@gmail.com from the address linked to your Google account. We will process the request within 30 days.

Children's privacy

The app is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Security

We use Google's secure infrastructure (Firebase, AdMob) for all data storage and transmission. Data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS. However, no online service is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of the page will reflect the latest version. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
